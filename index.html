Research Interest

 I'm a researcher of machine learning and data mining, especially on multi-view clustering, deep clustering and optimization theory. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Multi-view Clustering/Fusion
  • Deep clustering with Optimal Transport (OT) / Mutual Information (MI)
  • Optimization theory on Riemannian Manifold
  • Unsupervised graph representation learning
We have built a comprehensive Awesome-Deep-Multiview-Clustering-Repository (here) and an awesome-multi-view-clustering-repository (here). We have also built a data repository for large-scale data (here). Don't hesitate to contact me: jinjiaqi@nudt.edu.cn.
